About Us

Netflix is a leading global streaming service, founded in 1997 by Reed Hastings and Marc Randolph. Initially a DVD rental-by-mail business, it transitioned to streaming in 2007. With over 230 million subscribers in 190 countries, Netflix offers a vast library of TV shows, movies, documentaries, and original content. Notable originals include "Stranger Things," "The Crown," and "The Witcher." Its algorithm personalizes recommendations based on user preferences, enhancing the viewing experience. Netflix's diverse content and continuous innovation have made it a dominant player in the entertainment industry, shaping how audiences worldwide consume media.
